Planning

  • Derrick spoke about what the Eclipse Foundation is trying to achieve by creating a Roadmap and how project level planning fits in.

 

CDT 3.0

  • Project Milestones - the use of milestones was discussed. Several milestones would be set for the project. Developers could then target specific milestones for delivering their contributions. This would allow the project team to have a sense of progress and allow people to plan accordingly when they are dependant on a feature being complete in order to submit their work. It was also suggested that our milestones would reflect the eclipse 3.1 milestones, since we plan to release in sync with them.

 

  • It was also noted during the call that communication between projects needs to be improved, especially when they need to synchronize their releases. Any suggestions here would be appreciated.

 

  • Brent - CDT 2.0 shipped based on a date, not quality. Everyone seemed to agree that Quality objectives should be added .It was noted that CDT. 2.1 required that there be no open issues for the final release candidate. This allowed for open discussion of what would be fixed or not fixed. We should make this a policy for all future releases. This is a step in the right direction and we should look at what can be done better in the future.
