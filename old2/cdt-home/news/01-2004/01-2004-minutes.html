IBM-Rational:

  1.  Continue to work on 2.0 features and helping with fixing and committing patches that address 1.2 issues.
     
  2.  In the middle of creating design specs for all 2.0 feature work, will be done by end of January.

Redhat:

  1. Continuing work on hover help and profiling plugins, should be ready to commit very soon. A question came up about how the hover help will be structured given that its a little RedHat/Linux specific; the answer was that it will be in a separate plug in that it not part of the base CDT.
     
  2. Internal planning for 2004 being wrapped up, should have a clearer picture w.r.t. to CDT activities in 2004 soon.

 

Altera: Working on a product prototype based on CDT 1.2

 

Intel: No new update...

 

Real Time Innovations: Keeping themselves informed of CDT developments

 

CDT 1.2.1 status:

Continuing to find and fix some issues on the 1.2.1 RC1 build, but nothing worth holding off doing a GA build for wider adoption. A target was set for doing another release candidate build at the end of the following week (January 16, 2004).

 

CDT 2.0 Status:

  • The proposed Managed Build Enhancements feature will be been to the committed section of the 2.0 plan as IBM-Rational has committed the resources for the associated development, testing and documentation. See the CDT 2.0 Plan for more details.

     

  • We need to setup inbox mail accounts to help us better triage incoming defects, along the lines of what the Eclipse project does. Kleo has taken the action to request this via Webmaster, highlighting that this was discussed and agreed upon at the CDT monthly conference calls.

 

  • Eclipse Conference coming up first week of February 2004. Several folks from QNX, IBM, Intel, RTI and possibly others will be attending. CDT specific highlights will include a presentation/demonstration and also a "birds of a feather" session.

 

Documentation

As mentioned, Angelina Gomes is the new contact point for CDT documentation, taking over for Tanya-Marise De Sousa. Angeline will not be active in this role until approximately mid-February 2004. She will be monitoring the documentation mailing list and participating in some CDT design proposal meetings in the meantime in an effort to get up to speed as quickly as possible.

 

Verification/Bugs:

Not much to discuss here since we are in the early stages of the 2.0 release.

 

Infrastructure/Builds:

The CDT 2.0 automated build issues have been resolved, we are generating regular 2.0 builds again.


 

Any comments, additions, corrections etc. welcome! As always, many thanks to everyone for your ongoing support.
