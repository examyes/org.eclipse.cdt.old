CDT 2.0 Status

The end game plan for CDT 2.0 is to build a release candidate once the Eclipse platform has reached GA. That build is currently planned for Monday, June 28 at 3:00 am. Only show-stopper type bugs would be considered for fixing beyond that point. If no such defects come up after a few days of soak, we would declare GA.

 

CDT 2.0 Gating Bugs

The link below brings up a list of bugzilla defects that we have been calling gating. The criteria that is applied to determine whether a bug is "gating" or not is either of the following conditions:

 - The severity of the defect is blocker, critical, or major

- The priority of the defect is P1 or P2.

* Defects that have been marked for fixing in the future (target_milestone = 2.0.1, 2.1 or Future) are obviously NOT part of this gating list, even if their severity or priority is sufficiently high.
